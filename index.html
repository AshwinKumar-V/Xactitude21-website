Xactitude is an Annual Science and Technology festival organized by the Department of Computer Science (UG) of Kristu Jayanti College. It is one such platform where young brains get to showcase their skills and compete with each others to exhibit the best version of themselves. It is an event where new ideas are seen live. Year after year, Xactitude explores various aspects of science and technology and the profound impacts they have on our lives. It has constantly grown, evolved, and pushed the boundaries of what a college fest can be. With something to offer for everyone, Xactitude is an ideal destination for all technophiles to witness, learn and experience the wonders of science and technology.
